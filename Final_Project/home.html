Welcome to BREAKOUT

Here are the rules:

Bounce the ball off the walls, the cieling, the bricks, or your moveable paddle to remove all the bricks. Move your mouse to the left or to the right to change the positioning of the paddle. You have 3 chances to remove all of the bricks, can you do it? Once the game ends, you will then be able to add your username and score to the leaderboard.
